Home

SIF is a place where locals and internationals from all over the world come to worship God. We also want to be a “home away from home” for international students, supporting them and their families during their time in Stellenbosch.

Involvement

All of us are in it together. Worship God with your involvement in the Worship team, Discipleship, Evangelism, Service or Financial Stewardship

Activities

SIF has a number of weekly activities. Join us for Bible Study, Spice group (a support group for wives of international students), Sunday school, Prayer evening and much more. The SIF community also enjoys to do sports together, go to the beach, hikes or even camps during holidays.

Our core values

Love God (intimacy)
Love one another (community)
Love the lost (witness Christ)

Where

Services are held at Die Khaya (corner of Victoria and Ryneveld street). 'Khaya' means 'home' in isiXhosa.
Kruiskerk, Victoria Street, Stellenbosch, 7599

When

Join us on Sundays at 10:00 for a weekly worship service in English. All students, families and individuals are welcome! Stay for refreshments after the service and meet the rest of the fellowship.
Every Sunday morning at 10:30
